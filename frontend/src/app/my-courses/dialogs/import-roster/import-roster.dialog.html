Import From Canvas

Add students to a course section by importing the roster CSV from Canvas.

Steps to Download Roster CSV:

  1. Navigate to the "Grades" tab in your Canvas course page.
  2. On the top right, press the dropdown "Export".
  3. Select "Export Entire Gradebook".
  4. Now, fill in the prompts below to upload your roster into the CSXL site.
